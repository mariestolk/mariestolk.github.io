# About

This site is a collection of notes on topics I am exploring, including programming, puzzles, personal development, and anything else that catches my interest. I use it to document what I learn, clarify my own thinking, and share projects I am working on.

The site does not follow one fixed theme. Each post starts with something I wanted to understand a little better.
